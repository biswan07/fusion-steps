1. Getting Started

What is Fusion Steps?

Fusion Steps is your personal dance academy management app. It helps you manage your batches, track student attendance, handle class-pack subscriptions, and share dance videos — all from your phone or computer.

How to Access the App

1Open the App

Go to fusion-steps.netlify.app in any web browser (Chrome, Safari, etc.) on your phone or computer.

2Sign In

Enter your email address and password, then tap Sign In.

3Install on Your Phone (Optional)

For the best experience, add the app to your home screen:

On Android: Tap the browser menu (three dots) → "Add to Home Screen"
On iPhone: Tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen"

This makes the app work like a native app — it opens full-screen without the browser bar!

Once installed on your home screen, the app loads instantly and works even with a slow connection (you can view your data offline).

Navigation

The app has four main tabs at the bottom of the screen:

🏠
Home
Today's batches, alerts, recent uploads
📋
Batches
Create & manage dance batches
👥
Students
Add students, view profiles
🎬
Videos
Upload & manage class videos

3. Managing Batches

Batches are your dance classes — like "Wednesday Bollywood Beginners" or "Saturday Fusion Advanced."

Creating a New Batch

1Go to the Batches tab

Tap 📋 Batches in the bottom navigation.

2Tap "+ New Batch"

A form will appear at the top of the screen.

3Fill in the details

Batch Name: e.g., "Wednesday Bollywood Beginners"
Day of Week: Select the day this class runs
Time: e.g., "6:00 PM"
Style: Bollywood, Western, or Fusion
Level: Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced

4Tap "Create Batch"

The batch appears in your list immediately. Batches are colour-coded by style:

Orange = Bollywood    Teal = Western    Purple = Fusion

Viewing a Batch

Tap any batch in the list to see its detail page. You'll see:

  • Batch name, day, time, style, and level
  • Mark Attendance button
  • List of enrolled students (with their subscription balance)
  • Recent attendance records

4. Managing Students

Adding a New Student

1Go to the Students tab

Tap 👥 Students in the bottom navigation.

2Tap "+ Add Student"
3Enter their details

Full Name: Their display name
Email: They'll use this to log in
Phone: Optional

4Tap "Create Student"

The student account is created. They will receive a password reset email to set up their login.

The student must check their email and click the password reset link to set their password before they can log in.

Adding a Student to a Batch

1Open the student's profile

Tap their name in the Students list.

2Tap "Add to Batch"

A list of available batches will appear.

3Select the batch

They're now enrolled! They'll appear in that batch's student list and can see videos assigned to that batch.

Searching for a Student

Use the search bar at the top of the Students tab. Type any part of their name or email to filter the list instantly.

5. Marking Attendance

This is something you'll do every class. Here's how:

Quick Method (From Dashboard)

1Tap "Mark" on today's batch

On the Home screen, find today's batch and tap the orange Mark button.

From the Batch Detail Page

1Open the batch

Go to 📋 Batches → tap the batch → tap Mark Attendance.

Marking Students

2Tap each student who is present

Their row will turn green with a checkmark ✓. Everyone else is automatically marked absent.

Each student's row also shows their remaining class balance:

7 classes left — plenty remaining
4 classes left — getting low
1 class left — needs renewal soon

Students with 0 classes remaining will appear greyed out and cannot be marked present. They need a new class pack first.
3Review and confirm

Tap Submit Attendance. A confirmation dialog will show you how many students are present vs absent. Tap Confirm.

What Happens Automatically

ActionWhat Happens
Student marked present1 class is deducted from their pack. They get a push notification: "Your attendance for [Batch] on [Date] has been marked — Fusion Steps"
Balance reaches ≤ 2 classesStudent gets a low-balance notification: "You have 2 classes left — talk to Sriparna to top up"
Balance reaches 0The pack is automatically deactivated. Student can't be marked present until they get a new pack.
You can only mark attendance once per batch per day. If you try again, you'll see a message saying attendance has already been recorded.

6. Subscriptions & Class Packs

Students pay for class packs (5, 10, or 20 classes). When you mark attendance, 1 class is deducted automatically.

Assigning a Class Pack

1Open the student's profile

Go to 👥 Students → tap the student's name.

2Tap "Assign Pack"
3Choose a pack size

Select 5 Classes, 10 Classes, or 20 Classes.

4Tap "Assign Pack"

The student's balance is updated immediately. If they had an existing pack, it will be replaced by the new one.

Assign a new pack when the student pays you in person (cash or bank transfer). There's no online payment — you handle that outside the app.

Understanding the Balance Badge

Badge ColourMeaning
5+ classes leftAll good — no action needed
3-4 classes leftGetting low — student should plan to renew soon
0-2 classes leftUrgent — will run out soon. Shows on your Dashboard alerts too.

7. Video Library

Share class recordings, choreography breakdowns, and performance videos with your students.

Uploading a Video

1Go to the Videos tab

Tap 🎬 Videos → tap + Upload.

2Fill in the details

Title: e.g., "Nagada Sang Dhol — Full Choreo"
Description: Optional notes about the video
Style: Bollywood, Western, or Fusion
Visible to batches: Select which batches can see this video (tap to toggle)

3Select the video file

Tap the upload area. Supported formats: MP4, MOV, WebM. Maximum size: 500 MB.

4Tap "Upload Video"

A progress bar shows the upload status. Once complete, students in the selected batches will receive a push notification: "New video: [Title] — watch now!"

Filtering Videos

Use the style filter tabs at the top of the Videos page: All, Bollywood, Western, or Fusion.

Playing a Video

Tap any video card to play it inline. Tap Close to dismiss the player.

9. Troubleshooting

Common Issues

ProblemSolution
"Invalid email or password"Double-check your email spelling. If you forgot your password, ask Sriparna to send a new password reset link.
Student can't log inMake sure they clicked the password reset link in their email and set a password. Check spam/junk folders.
Can't mark a student presentTheir class balance is at 0. Assign a new class pack first.
"Attendance already recorded"You already submitted attendance for this batch today. Each batch can only have attendance marked once per day.
Videos not showing for a studentMake sure the student is enrolled in a batch that has access to that video. Check their profile → Batches.
App is slow or not loadingPull down to refresh, or close and reopen the app. Check your internet connection.
"You're offline" banner showingYour device has no internet. You can still view cached data, but can't make changes until you're back online.
